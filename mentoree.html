Mentoree Program

We are excited to extend an opportunity that could transform your career and empower your journey in the world of technology. At The City of Refuge, we are dedicated to nurturing your skills and guiding you towards a brighter future through our mentorship program.

Are you ready to embrace a career in the ever-evolving tech industry? Whether your heart is set on becoming a cyber security expert safeguarding digital realms or a web development wizard crafting immersive online experiences, we are here to help you reach your goals. Our mentorship program is designed to provide you with the support and resources you need to succeed. Here's what we offer:

  1. One-on-One Guidance: You'll be paired with a dedicated mentor who will walk beside you on this journey, offering personalized advice and expertise.
  2. Tailored Curriculum: We have carefully crafted curricula for both cyber security and web development, ensuring you receive the relevant training and skills necessary for your chosen path.
  3. Community: Join a network of like-minded individuals who share your faith and aspirations, providing support and camaraderie throughout your journey.
  4. Scholarships and Financial Aid: We understand the financial challenges many face. We offer scholarships and financial aid to eligible mentorees to ease the burden of pursuing certification.
  5. Job Placement Assistance: Upon completing your certification, we will assist you in finding job opportunities and connecting with potential employers in the tech industry.

Now is the time to turn your dreams into reality and embark on a meaningful career in tech. Let us be your guiding light in this exciting adventure.

If you're ready to take the next step and join our mentorship program, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. Together, we can achieve greatness in the tech field. To express your interest or learn more by filling out the form below.
